Literature Society MCKL

Jun 1, 2022 · Sidney Soh

Founded the Society in May 2022

My growing passion in the world of literature inspired me to found and be the President of the first ever Literature Society in MCKL. The society gained over 50 club members in the span of a year, and we conducted several society meetups where club members would band together and discuss certain works of literature, including movies, books and poems.

As President, my responsibilities included delegating tasks to my fellow committee members, leading discussions during committee meetings, and overseeing all of the society activities. I played an important role in keeping the society active, and then personally recruited new committee members to continue the legacy of the society in years to come.

Society Event

On November 11th 2022, Literature Society collaborated with Psychology Society and hosted a Murder Mystery Event for our club members. Our event planning team created the murder mystery storyline, planned and created the props, and designed the riddles and clues for the event.
